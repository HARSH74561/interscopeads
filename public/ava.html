Ava Finance

Converting U.S. Users into Paid Subscribers with High Funnel Efficiency

App:
Ava Finance
Platform:
Android & iOS
Geo:
United States
Model:
CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)

Objectives

The campaign aimed to acquire high-intent U.S. users and guide them through a conversion-optimized funnel from install to paid subscription, while minimizing churn and maximizing compliance.

Strategy

  • Full-funnel performance acquisition model
  • Targeted channels: TikTok, Meta, YouTube Shorts, Google Search
  • Deep linking to streamline onboarding and improve CVR
  • Strict adherence to funnel compliance & quality policies

Results

  • Install-to-Subscribe CVR: 17% across top sources
  • 7-Day Cancellation Rate: 7.5% (within goal)
  • Top Channels: YouTube Shorts & Meta (retargeting)
  • Traffic Quality: Fully compliant, clean traffic only
