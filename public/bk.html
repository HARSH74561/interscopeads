Burger King

Driving Verified Purchase Deliveries Through High-Intent Media

App:
Burger King
Platform:
Android & iOS
Geo:
Russia
Model:
CPA – Pay-per-Delivery

Campaign

The Burger King campaign focused on acquiring non-incentivized users who complete verified delivery purchases. We implemented strict compliance and fraud monitoring systems while optimizing for performance KPIs like Install-to-Purchase IR and CTIT thresholds.

Strategy

  • Pre-vetted DSPs and in-app placements
  • Advertiser-approved creatives for brand consistency
  • Source-level monitoring with throttling against fraud

Results

  • CTIT >60 mins: 7.5% (Below threshold)
  • Install-to-Purchase IR: 0.045% (Above goal)
  • Post-Attribution Fraud: 9.6% (Well within limit)
