Campaign

The Careem Food campaign was designed to drive first-time food orders through a performance-driven full-funnel strategy. By leveraging real-time data, localized creatives, and predictive targeting, we delivered cost-effective installs and consistent order completions across key Middle Eastern markets.

Strategy

  • Cross-Channel Media Buying (in-app, DSPs, influencers, OEM)
  • Real-time event optimization via Supabase & MMP
  • Targeted food app switchers and high-intent users
  • 300+ localized ad variants with A/B testing
  • Predictive user models for D30 conversion

Results

  • D30 Food Order Cohort Conversion Rate: 6%
  • D30 Complete Transaction Rate: 5%
  • Maintained AOV across markets: $40–$50
  • Outcome: High-intent registrations and strong re-order signals
