Noon

Powering Verified First-Time Orders in the UAE via High-Intent App Performance

App:
noon: Shopping, Food, Grocery
Platform:
Android & iOS
Geo:
United Arab Emirates
Model:
CPO (Cost Per Order)

Campaign

The goal was to drive high-quality installs for Noon that converted into first-time and repeat purchase events, validated through Google Analytics and CRM. Fulfilled, non-returned orders were strictly counted as valid conversions.

Strategy

  • Used pre-approved, high-performing media sources
  • Localized creatives targeting high-intent UAE users
  • Excluded Grocery & Minutes verticals per brand policy
  • Matched GA session and CRM data for validation
  • Maintained install-to-order visibility with CR thresholds

Results

  • Click-to-Install CR: 0.37% (Above 0.3% KPI)
  • Install CR (Threshold): Maintained > 0.06%
  • Conversion Validation: GA & CRM matched orders
  • Fulfillment Rate: 92% of purchases delivered
  • Top Creatives: UAE festival offers, fast delivery & deals
