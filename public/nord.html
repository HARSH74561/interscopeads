NordVPN

Driving Premium Subscriptions Through Strictly Compliant CPA Campaigns

App:
NordVPN – Fast & Secure VPN
Platforms:
iOS & Android
Geo:
United States
Model:
CPA – Pay-per-Purchase

Campaign

The NordVPN campaign aimed to acquire premium paid users in the U.S. through performance-driven channels with strict transparency and brand-compliant practices. Emphasis was placed on yearly plans to increase LTV and subscription value.

Strategy

  • Device-specific targeting for iOS and Android to optimize performance
  • Approved placements only for clean traffic and brand-safe environments
  • Audience focus: digital nomads, privacy seekers, remote workers
  • Creative focus on annual savings, privacy, and streaming freedom
  • Source-level transparency with real-time dashboard tracking

Results

  • Click-to-Install CR: 1.12% (Above target)
  • Install-to-Purchase CR: 2.05% avg. (Android > iOS)
  • Higher AOV: via yearly plan conversions
  • Traffic Compliance: Full source transparency & naming integrity
  • Outcome: Campaign cleared for scale-up due to quality performance
