Swiggy Instamart

Accelerating First Orders Across Tier-1 & Tier-2 Indian Cities

App:
Swiggy Instamart
Platform:
Android & iOS
Geo:
India
Model:
CPFT (Cost per First Transaction)

Campaign

The Swiggy Instamart campaign aimed to drive high-quality first orders across India's Tier-1 and Tier-2 cities using regional targeting, engaging creatives, and strict attribution compliance.

Strategy

  • 45-city focused in-app and programmatic placements
  • Targeted Urban Gen-Z, professionals, and impulse buyers
  • Localized banners with discounts and “10-min delivery” visuals
  • Anti-fraud filters to block hijacking/flooding

Results

  • CTR: 0.6%+ across all placements
  • AOV: ₹225 average
  • Repeat Orders (M0): 22%
  • Attribution Rejection Rate: <1%
  • CRM Reporting: Fully aligned with backend validations
